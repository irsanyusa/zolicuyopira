Coachella Announces 2015 Lineup | TIME

July 2024 · 1 minute read

January 6, 2015 2:40 PM EST

Start packing up your day-glo fanny packs — the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival announced its highly anticipated lineup Tuesday on Twitter. It was retweeted more than 16,000 times in an hour.

The festival will take place over the course of two three day weekends, April 10-12 and April 17-19, in Indio, Calif. AC/DC, Jack White and Drake will headline the Friday, Saturday and Sunday shows, respectively. Other big names include Interpol, Steely Dan, Bell and Sebastian, Florence + the Machine, and Kaskade, to name a few.
