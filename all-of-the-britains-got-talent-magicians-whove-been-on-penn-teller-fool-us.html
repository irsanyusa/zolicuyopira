Over the years of existence of Penn & Teller: Fool Us, a number of magicians from Britain’s Got Talent have joined the series. In an attempt to fool Penn Jillette and Raymond Teller, these illusionists tried to execute their magic tricks in the most seamless way possible. Some have successfully done so, while there were also others who failed.

We listed Britain’s Got Talent magicians who appeared on Penn & Teller: Fool Us. If you want to take a look at their incredible performances, keep scrolling down.

Britain’s Got Talent Magicians Who Have Been on Penn & Teller: Fool Us

1. Ryan Hayashi

After he joined season four of Britain’s Got Talent, samurai sword act Ryan Hayashi appeared on Fool Us season five. His performance consisted of an over-the-top coin matrix, saying he’s showing the basics of magic. Apparently, his impressive trick act was listed by Geek.com as one of the best magic tricks from the show.

2. Kat Hudson

Kat Hudson appeared on Fool Us after she participated in season 14 of Britain’s Got Talent. During her performance, she invited four random people to get an item from her blue handbag and hide them from her. Impressively, she managed to guess all the items correctly as if she read the minds of the volunteers.

3. Darcy Oake

After reaching the Finale of BGT season eight, Darcy Oake then joined Penn & Teller: Fool Us in August 2018. During his performance, he made Penn Jilette select a random card from a deck and made the same vanish and reappear inside a bottle locked in a box. Unfortunately, he failed to fool the duo amid his act. 

4. Mr. Cuddles

Prior to joining BGT season 14, ventriloquist Mr. Cuddles competed on Fool Us 2018. He executed his mind reading trick alongside his puppet Evil Octopus, making Alyson Hannigan choose a sock from a bag and revealing its pair to be inside his cosmetics brain. Consequently, Mr. Cuddles became the first ever ventriloquist-magician to have successfully fooled the duo.

5. Alan Hudson

Before Alan Hudson competed on series 14 of BGT, he graced the first season of Fool Us, then returned to the show over a decade later. The comedic magician’s act consisted of finding Alyson Hannigan’s signed playing card while he’s wrapped in plastic wrap.

6. Wayne Hoffman

Ahead of BGT season 12, Wayne Hoffman joined season three of Fool Us. Back in his 2016 performance, he has successfully fooled the duo by performing a Mind Candy Club membership routine.
