Olajiide solidifies status as Asakes protg with latest single No Story

June 2024 · 2 minute read

The uplifting track, which dropped on all major streaming platforms yesterday, showcases Olajiide's growth and artistry, further establishing his connection to Asake's musical legacy. With "No Story", Olajiide reminds listeners that success is within reach, encouraging perseverance and hope.

In a statement, Olajiide shared his inspiration for the song, saying, "I believe that the universe rewards us all in time, and that success is not a competition - there's room for everyone to thrive." With "No Story", Olajiide cements his status as a rising star in the Afrobeats scene and a worthy protégé of Asake.

Notably, Olajiide has absorbed Asake's signature Fuji infusion, incorporating the genre's percussive rhythms and melodic patterns into his own music. This blend of styles has resulted in a unique sound that is both nostalgic and innovative. Additionally, Olajiide has adopted Asake's storytelling approach, weaving vivid narratives into his songs.

By combining these elements with his own soulful vocals and contemporary production, Olajiide has created a sound that is both authentic and forward-thinking. Industry insiders have praised Olajiide's latest effort, noting his signature soulful vocals and catchy melody.
