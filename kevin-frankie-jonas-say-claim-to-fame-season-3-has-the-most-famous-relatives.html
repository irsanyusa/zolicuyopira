Kevin and Frankie Jonas recently appeared on Good Morning America to tease the upcoming third season of Claim of Fame on ABC. The two brothers host the reality competition show, in which the lesser-known relatives of celebrities try to guess each other’s famous families.

Kevin, Frankie Jonas Tease Claim to Fame Season 3

Frankie described Claim to Fame as the “Nepo Baby Olympics,” as the relatives of celebrities try to keep their identities secret from each other. Kevin shared that even he and Frankie don’t know who the contestants are related to.

“But this season is unlike any other season,” Kevin teased. “They came in full disguises, full accents. They are now playing the game … Now they’re like, ‘I’m gonna come in there and be a completely different person.'”

Kevin also revealed that in this upcoming season, “the celebrity relatives’ talent is the highest it’s ever been.” He added that he “can honestly say it’s the most famous people ever” whose relatives are competing on this season.

What Is It Like for the Brothers to Work Together?

Kevin joked that there could be a separate show about him and Frankie “trying to put up with each other during the show.” More seriously, he shared that he loves working with his brother. He pointed out that they’re 13 years apart, so this has given them a new opportunity to bond.

When asked what clues Frankie would give about Kevin being his famous relative, he responded that Kevin “does a signature move where he spins the guitar around his chest.” So he would show a swinging guitar. He also might show some mutton chops, which Kevin sported “back in the day.”

You can watch the new season of Claim to Fame on Wednesday nights this summer at 9/8c on ABC. Are you excited for the new episodes? Let us know in the comments below.
