Looking For a Family Vacation This Summer? Hiltons All-Inclusive Resorts Have You Covered

July 2024 · 7 minute read

It’s officially May, which means it’s time to begin booking spring and summer travel — and successfully get your family vacation out of the group chat.

Finding the right family vacation, which should offer enough stimulation and activities for the kids, but rejuvinating relaxation for adults, can feel like an ominous task. Thankfully, Hilton‘s wide array of all-inclusive resorts has you covered.

Their gorgeous properties, with locations spanning from the the pristine shores of the Caribbean coastline to the secluded stretches of the Mayan coastline, boast kids and teen clubs, family suites and water park-like pools. But international bars and restaurants, wine tasting excursions, and luxury spa services mean the adults are never forgotten about.

Below, check out Hilton’s best all-inclusive resorts to bring the family this year:

Hilton Tulum Riviera Maya All-Inclusive Resort

BOOK Now

Nestled along the pristine shores of the Caribbean’s coastline, the Hilton Tulum Riviera Maya All-Inclusive Resort is one of, if not the best, family-friendly resorts. The all-inclusive resort has facilities for every age group, including a vibrant kids club catered towards children aged 4-12, featuring an outdoor playground and scheduled activities such as piñata making and mask decorating. Teens aren’t left out either, with a dedicated club offering a range of activities from water volleyball to video game showdowns. 
 
Their spacious Family Suites, designed with kids in mind, are located closer to the property’s family-friendly amenities such as the sparkling pools, water slides and the Kids Club. Exclusive suite amenities include large building blocks, a teepee, toys, sunblock and bath toiletries for kids, a teddy bear and coloring books

For adults seeking relaxation and culinary delights, the resort boasts an international bar and the expansive buffet at Vela Sur, where gastronomic delights await around the clock. Looking for a sweet treat? An adorable ice cream and churro shop is just a skip away from one of the resort’s nine pools, each offering its own unique ambiance, from a tranquil oasis for the adults to a lively hub for the whole family.  

For a taste of Mexico’s rich viticulture, parents can embark on a guided journey with a sommelier, accompanied by delectable hors d’oeuvres inspired by the country’s renowned wine regions. And for a lazier day, 16 treatment rooms and hydro massage therapy await amidst the lush tropical forest. Meanwhile, fitness enthusiasts can break a sweat at the on-site gym or partake in beachside group classes led by certified instructors. 

Most extraordinary are the resort’s Enclave rooms, which include private check-in and check-out, access to the Enclave Lounge for more private dining and pool experiences, and charming in-room welcome amenities and more thoughtful perks that will make your stay feel even more special. 

Hilton Cancun, An All-Inclusive Resort

BOOK Now

Nestled between Cancun and the quiet fishing village of Puerto Morelos, set on a secluded stretch of Mayan Coastline, the Hilton Cancun offers a quieter escape for families. The all-inclusive resort, which first opened in November 2021, features 715 stylish guest rooms – all offering panoramic views of nature and the ocean, including the resort’s sought-after Swim-Up Poolside rooms, featuring a pool connected directly to the patio. 

The headlining feature of Hilton Cancun, however, is their wide range of culinary experiences. The property has 12 all-inclusive, authentic, globally inspired restaurants, including five signature restaurants, a kid-friendly all-you-can-eat ice cream and churro shop, and a taqueria with a mezcal and tequila bar.  

While the kids busy themselves at the splash pool or with games and activities at the kids club, adults can relax at the Waldorf Astoria Spa Cancun. Just next door to the resort, the unique spa blends innovative treatments with holistic Mayan traditions. The spa offers 21 treatment rooms, both inside and outside, that incorporate fresh garden herbs, locally sourced oils, and healing crystals.

Hilton La Romana, An All-Inclusive Resort

BOOK Now

You also can’t go wrong with the white sand and blue water of Hilton La Romana, An All-Inclusive Resort in the Dominican Republic, which boasts both family-friendly and adults-only resorts depending on what type of vacation you’re looking for.  

For families, the best option for lodging is the all-Inclusive Family Resort nestled in the lush jungles, gorgeous weather and expansive beaches of the island. The resort’s headlining perks come with their family-friendly daily offerings, including a water park features winding tube slides, a palm tree-lined lazy river, and a kids’ splash zone; free kayaking, snorkeling, and windsurfing sessions; and supervised activities at their kids/teens clubs, such as  treasure hunts, sandcastle building, beach camping, foam parties, bonfires and a gaming room. 

And if you enjoyed your stay and want to go back without the kiddos? Look no further than Hilton La Romana’s All-Inclusive Adults-Only Resort. The luxury retreat features oversized guest room (stocked with international and domestic top-shelf spirits and an in-suite mini-bar), gourmet dining offered at five different restaurants (no reservations required), infinity pools, ocean-front bars, a day spa, nightly entertainment, a casino, a tree-lined private beach, and a dive center.
