20 Astonishing Dogs That Seem to Have Come From Another Planet

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Once in a blue moon, you might get lucky enough to encounter a dog whose characteristics are unlike any dog you’ve ever seen. Maybe a good boy with freckles or a good girl with a mane like a lion’s, who knows? What’s certain is these dogs are special and deserve to be seen and adored by the world.

Bright Side now comes to you with a collection of dogs that captivate everyone who looks at them. Their otherworldly beauty will leave you even more fascinated with man’s best friend.

1. The beauty of this cross between a Husky and a Pitbull will take your breath away instantly.

2. “One sec, almost done putting on my eyeliner!”

3. This freckled good boy definitely deserves the world.

4. Zuko was born with a skin condition which just makes him stand out from the other Labrador angels out there.

5. Bloomin is a rare tawny colored, blue eyed Pug who will win your heart in a matter of seconds.

6. His name is Brick, and he’s an 11-year-old Rottweiler who rocks white fur instead of black because of vitiligo.

7. Charlie the Dalmatian is giving you heart eyes, literally!

8. This dog could have easily played Antonio Banderas’ dog in the movie Zorro.

9. Maybe the printer ran out of ink...

10. Mismatched teeny-tiny French Bulldog paws with the power to make you feel warm inside

11. An American Bulldog you’ll have to stare at for a while to be able to really take in his uniqueness

12. The boy with the piercing eyes is Ekko and his vitiligo just increases our love for him.

13. A French Bulldog puppy that blows up all the cuteness scales with its blue eyes and merle fur

14. You probably don’t see Bergamasco Shepherds often, so take some time to fully appreciate their good looks.

15. Prague, the Pitbull, is very often compared to a Black Panther because of his rare jet-black coat.

16. Who wouldn’t want to have a dog that looks different every time you look at them from another angle?

17. A very special, extra gorgeous Bernese Mountain dog you’ll be thinking about for a long time

18. The Mudi is a herding dog breed from Hungary who will enchant you with their wavy fur.

19. At first glance this might look like the Lion King, but it’s really just a giant Tibetan Mastiff.

20. The Xoloitzcuintli is better known as the Mexican Hairless dog and will steal your heart whether you’re ready for it or not.

Which of these good boys and girls surprised you the most? Pour all the love you might have for them in the comments because they deserve nothing less.
