Lizzo Sparks Breakup Rumors With Boyfriend Myke Wright Amid Legal Turmoil

June 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Dave Benett/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Fame often casts an unforgiving spotlight on even the most private aspects of celebrities’ lives. Such is the case with the Grammy-winning artist Lizzo, whose relationship with Myke Wright has recently come under scrutiny amid her ongoing legal battles. As fans speculate about the status of their relationship, the couple’s social media activity has raised questions. It is also further aggravated by Lizzo’s legal troubles.

Lizzo, Myke Wright No Longer Follow Each Other

Eagle-eyed fans have been quick to notice changes in Lizzo and Wright’s social media presence. A swift comparison revealed that the duo, who had been consistently present on each other’s Instagram profiles, no longer follow each other.

Moreover, their collective photos seem to have been removed from their respective accounts. They suggest a potential shift in their relationship dynamics. However, a spokesperson for Lizzo was swift to dismiss any breakup rumors, insisting that there was “no truth” to the speculations.

Documenting their night out at the Gov Ball in New York City, Lizzo and Wright offered a glimpse into their connection. Their shared photos from the event spoke volumes about their bond and seemed to reaffirm their commitment to each other.

However, the current state of their relationship remains a matter of speculation. The couple’s next steps will undoubtedly be closely followed by their fan base. Their journey highlights the challenges that celebrities face in safeguarding their personal lives from public scrutiny.

Lizzo is Being Accused of Harassment

The rumors of the possible breakup emerge amid of Lizzo’s ongoing legal woes. Accused of sexual harassment, religious harassment, and creating a hostile work environment, the artist finds herself embroiled in a lawsuit filed by former dancers.

Allegations against her, her production company, and dance captain Shirlene Quigley have cast a shadow over her career. In response, Lizzo issued a statement vehemently denying the claims. While also emphasizing her commitment to professionalism and respect for her employees.

Wright has remained notably silent amidst the legal turmoil. The couple’s relationship, which began in 2021, has been underpinned by Wright’s supportive presence. In an earlier interview, Lizzo praised Wright’s unwavering support for her career. Also highlighting the importance of mutual support in a relationship.

The challenges of maintaining a relationship amidst a high-profile legal battle are not to be underestimated. Lizzo’s journey through fame and controversy certainly underscores the need for a solid support system. Wright’s steadfastness as someone who knew her before her rise to stardom has likely been a source of strength.
