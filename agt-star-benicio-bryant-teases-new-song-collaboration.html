America’s Got Talent Season 14 finalist Benicio Bryant has teased an upcoming song called “Move to Mars.” The new project is in collaboration with singer Audrey Mika, and fans can’t wait to hear it.

Benicio Bryant Teases New Song ‘Move to Mars’

On Monday, Bryant took to social media to share a teaser for his new song “Move to Mars.” The clip features footage of Bryant and Mika in matching white jumpsuits and heavy boots, holding helmets and posing in a rocky locale reminiscent of Mars.

The teaser also includes a brief snippet of what is presumably the new song, although it’s hard to hear the lyrics clearly. Bryant wrote in the caption of his post that the track will be available on July 21, and fans can pre-save it now.

Bryant has put out several singles since appearing on AGT in 2019. Last year, he also shared the demo for a song called “Melancholy,” and he’s teased some other original music with fans on social media in recent months. He’s also posted some covers of songs by various artists.

Bryant Made It to the AGT Finals

Bryant competed on Season 14 of America’s Got Talent, auditioning when he was just 14 years old. The singer previously came in second place on The Voice Kids in Germany. For his AGT audition, he sang “The Joke” by Brandi Carlile.

For the rest of his time on the show, Bryant performed original songs. He earned enough votes from the viewers at home to make it to the Finals. However, he didn’t make it into the Top 5. But that didn’t stop him from signing a record deal after the show and embarking on a music career.
